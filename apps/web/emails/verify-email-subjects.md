# Gravitre Email Verification - Subject Line Options

## Recommended (Professional & Clear)
1. **Verify your Gravitre account**
2. **Confirm your email to get started with Gravitre**

## Alternative Options
3. **Action required: Verify your email address**
4. **Welcome to Gravitre - Please verify your email**
5. **One step left: Confirm your Gravitre account**

---

## Usage Notes

- **Option 1** is the most concise and professional - recommended for most cases
- **Option 2** adds context about getting started, good for onboarding flow
- **Option 3** creates urgency with "Action required" prefix
- **Option 4** leads with welcome messaging, warmer tone
- **Option 5** emphasizes how close they are to completing signup

## Supabase Configuration

In your Supabase dashboard:
1. Go to **Authentication** → **Email Templates**
2. Select **Confirm signup**
3. Set **Subject** to your chosen option
4. Paste the HTML template in the **Body** field
5. Save changes
